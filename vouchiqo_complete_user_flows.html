Phase 1 — Discovery and signup

Daily operations
1
New merchant signs up
Admin is notified. Reviews application — verifies business is legitimate.
Checks business name, website, category, GSTIN if provided
Approves → merchant gets full dashboard access
Rejects → sends reason, merchant can reapply
Approve → live Reject → notified
2
Manages the Hot Deals ticker
Ticker Management section — the platform's most valuable real estate.
Views current ticker order: paid slots → Enterprise → Pro → Growth → Starter
Can manually pin or unpin any specific deal, overriding auto-priority
Sees which merchants have paid for featured placement + expiry dates
Can override the automatic order for specific campaigns or partnerships
3
Processes expired coupon revival requests
The Revival queue is admin's most time-sensitive task.
Revival request arrives with: coupon code, brand name, customer email
Admin contacts the merchant (email / dashboard notification)
Merchant decides within 48 hours — approve, reject, or offer alternative
Admin relays outcome to customer and updates revival status
Approved → customer gets working code Alternative → merchant sends new offer Rejected → customer notified

The expired coupon revival — step by step
1
Customer finds an expired coupon
Could be from anywhere — Google search, old email, a friend, or even the Expired tab on a brand page.
On /brand/[name] → Expired tab: each expired deal has "Request Revival" button
On homepage: dedicated Revival section with inline mini-form
Navbar: "Revive Coupon" link takes them to /expired-coupon-revival
Footer: "Expired Coupon Revival" is the first Quick Links item
2
Submits the revival form
Three fields — zero cost to the customer.
Expired coupon code (text input)
Brand / store name (with autocomplete from merchant database)
Customer email (to receive the outcome)
On submit: "Submitted! We'll reach out within 48 hours" inline success state
3
Vouchiqo admin receives the request
Appears in admin revival queue with customer details and the expired code.
Admin looks up the merchant in the system
Contacts merchant via dashboard notification or direct email
Explains: "A customer has this expired code and wants to complete a purchase"
4
Merchant receives revival request
Gets a notification in their merchant dashboard. Has 48 hours to decide.
Sees: customer's intended purchase, the expired code, request date
Business decision: this is a near-lost sale — good incentive to say yes
Approve: extend the code Counter: offer a different deal Reject: no extension possible
5
Outcome delivered to customer (within 48h)
Email sent to the address provided. Three possible results.
Approved → customer gets a fresh working code, valid for a short window
Counter → merchant sends an alternative discount or newer offer
Rejected → customer notified, encouraged to check active deals instead
✓
Everyone wins (78% of the time)
Customer saves money. Merchant recovers a sale they would have lost. Vouchiqo builds trust and loyalty as the platform that "went further".
This flow is Vouchiqo's strongest retention hook — no other coupon platform does this
